2D & 3D Shapes

Let's explore the amazing world of shapes! Learn about 2D shapes like circles and squares, and 3D shapes like cubes and spheres. We'll discover how shapes are everywhere around us!

ü§î What are Shapes?

Shapes are everywhere! Look around you - your desk is probably a rectangle, your pencil might be a cylinder, and the sun looks like a circle! Shapes help us understand the world around us.

2D Shapes are flat shapes that you can draw on paper. They have length and width, but no height.

3D Shapes are solid shapes that have length, width, and height. You can hold them in your hands!
